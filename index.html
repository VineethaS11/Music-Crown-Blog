Lewis Floyd Henry preps new mashup cover album

Emerging Talent,June 5th, 2018

Lewis Floyd Henry, a talented street performer, has announced the release of his new record, Mashup Album Vol. 1. The new album is an 8-bit soaked offering which features a collection of 10 mashups and cover tracks of artists such as The Notorious B.I.G., Giggs, AC/DC, ODB, Black Sabbath and more.
